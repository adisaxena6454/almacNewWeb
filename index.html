HOW WE WORKS

Few easy steps to get
interior design

The design of your kitchen varies from one layout to another. The shape of the kitchen also determines on the space for cabinets, countertops, and accessories.

01

Planning and Measurements:

* Assess your kitchen space and determine the layout and design you want for your modular kitchen.
* Take accurate measurements of the kitchen area, including walls, windows, doors, electrical points, plumbing connections, and any other relevant features.

02

Budgeting and Material Selection:

* Set a budget for your modular kitchen project, considering factors like materials, appliances, accessories, and installation costs.
* Choose the materials you want for your kitchen cabinets, countertops, backsplash, flooring, and hardware. Common options include wood, laminate, stainless steel, granite, quartz, and more.

03

Design and Customization:

* Consult with a kitchen designer or utilize design software to create a detailed plan for your modular kitchen.
* Decide on the cabinet layout, storage options, appliance placement, and other personalized features based on your needs and preferences.

04

Order and Delivery:

* Finalize your design and place an order for the modular kitchen components, including cabinets, countertops, and accessories, from a reputable supplier or manufacturer.
* Coordinate the delivery schedule to ensure the items arrive on time for installation.

05

Site Preparation:

* Clear out your existing kitchen and remove any old cabinets, appliances, and fixtures.
* Make necessary repairs to walls, floors, plumbing, and electrical connections to ensure they are in good condition.

06

Installation:

* Start with the installation of the modular kitchen cabinets, following the design plan and measurements.
* Fit the countertops, backsplash, and other components as per the design.
* Install kitchen appliances such as the stove, refrigerator, dishwasher, and microwave.

07

Plumbing and Electrical Work:

* Connect the plumbing lines to the sink, faucet, and any other fixtures.
* Ensure electrical connections are made for lighting, outlets, and appliances, adhering to safety standards and regulations. It's advisable to hire a professional for this step if you're not experienced with electrical work.

08

Finishing Touches:

* Install the kitchen accessories, handles, knobs, and pulls.
* Make any necessary adjustments, alignments, or minor modifications to ensure everything fits and functions properly.
